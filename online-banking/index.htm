How to Open a Bank Account Online
through Creaxus

What do I need to open a bank account?

To open a bank account online, there are a few basic requirements. Here are three things you’ll need to start opening your account:

  1. Social Security number
  2. Valid home address
  3. 18+ U.S. Citizen or resident of 50 United States

How do I open a mobile and online banking account through Creaxus?

Skip the branches, lines, and paperwork that some traditional banks require to open a checking account and apply instead for a new bank account online.  Creaxus makes the process of opening a bank account online easy. Here’s how to get started:

  • Visit member.Creaxus.com/enroll/ and complete the enrollment form. Opening an online banking account through Creaxus takes less than 2 minutes.
  • Once enrollment is completed, we’ll send a Creaxus Visa® Debit Card in the mail to the address you provided. Your new debit card typically arrives in 7-10 business days.
  • Download our mobile banking app, and log in. You can connect your existing bank account to transfer funds or set up direct deposit  You can also login to online banking at Creaxus.com when you need it.
Banking services provided by The Bancorp Bank or Stride Bank, N.A.; Members FDIC

Does opening bank account through Creaxus require a deposit?

Applying for a Creaxus Checking Account online is free with no opening deposit required.

Is a credit check required to open an account online?

Creaxus requires no credit check to open an account and provides services that are inclusive of all Americans. This includes those who struggle with bad credit history but are still looking for checking or savings accounts they can open and use entirely online with no opening deposit. Creaxus offers a Checking Account that provides a second chance to rebuild your financial health because it doesn’t rely on consumer credit reporting agencies like ChexSystems.
